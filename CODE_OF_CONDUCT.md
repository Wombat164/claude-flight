# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.

We commit to fostering an environment that respects the dignity and rights of all
individuals, regardless of characteristics including race, ethnicity, caste, color,
age, physical characteristics, neurodiversity, disability, sex or gender, gender
identity or expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophy or religion,
national or social origin, socio-economic position, level of education, or other
status.

## Encouraged Behaviors

1. Respecting the purpose of the community, its activities, and gathering methods
2. Engaging kindly and honestly with others
3. Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
4. Taking responsibility for our actions and contributions
5. Gracefully giving and accepting constructive feedback
6. Committing to repairing harm when it occurs
7. Behaving in ways that promote and sustain the well-being of the community

## Restricted Behaviors

1. **Harassment** -- violating expressed boundaries or continuing personal attention after a clear request to stop
2. **Character attacks** -- insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments toward individuals or groups
3. **Stereotyping or discrimination** -- characterizing behavior based on immutable identities or traits
4. **Sexualization** -- inappropriately intimate behavior for the community context
5. **Violating confidentiality** -- sharing personal or private information without permission
6. **Endangerment** -- causing, encouraging, or threatening violence or harm
7. Behaving in ways that threaten the well-being of the community
8. **Misleading identity** -- impersonating others or evading enforcement actions
9. **Failing to credit sources**, sharing off-norm **promotional materials**, or **irresponsible communication**

## Reporting an Issue

This is a small, solo-maintained project. Report a Code of Conduct concern
**privately** to the maintainer by opening a confidential report via this
repository's **Security advisories** -- it is the maintainer's private,
non-public contact channel:
<https://github.com/Wombat164/claude-flight/security/advisories/new>

Reports are taken seriously and handled promptly, privately, and as
confidentially as the situation allows.

## Addressing and Repairing Harm

Responses follow this enforcement ladder, scaled to severity:

1. **Warning** -- a single or minor incident -> private written warning; repair via a private apology and acknowledgement.
2. **Temporarily Limited Activities** -- repeated minor or a first serious violation -> private warning with a time-limited cooldown.
3. **Temporary Suspension** -- a pattern of repeated or a single serious violation -> removal for a period, with conditions for return.
4. **Permanent Ban** -- an unresolved pattern or an extremely serious violation -> complete, permanent removal from community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an
individual is officially representing the community in public or other spaces.
